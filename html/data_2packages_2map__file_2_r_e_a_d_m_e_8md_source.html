1 # map_file package
2 ## points_map_filter
3 ### feature
4 points_map_filter_node subscribe pointcloud maps and current pose, the node extract pointcloud near to the current pose.
5 
6 #### subscribed topics
7 /points_map (sensor_msgs/PointCloud2) : Raw pointcloud map. This topic usually comes from points_map_loader.
8 /current_pose (geometry_msgs/PoseStamped) : Current pose of the car. This topic usually comes from pose_relay node.
9 
10 #### published topics
11 /points_map/filtered (sensor_msgs/PointCloud2) : Filtered pointcloud submap.
12 
13 #### parameters
14 load_grid_size (double) : grid size of submap.
15 load_trigger_distance (double) : if the car moves load_trigger_distance(m), the map filter publish filtered submap.
16 
17 ### how it works
18 map_filter_node relay /points_map topic until it recieves /current_pose topic.
19 Then, the /current_pose topic recieved, the map_filter_node publish submap.

1 # Range Vision Fusion
2 
3 The Range Vision Fusion node will try match the objects detected on a range sensor, with the ones obtained from a vision detector.
4 A match will be considered found if the 3D projection of the object overlaps at least 50% (configurable) over the 2D object detection.
5 The label from the 2D Image detector will be attached to the corresponding 3D Object. All the matched results will be published.
6 
7 ## Requirements
8 
9 ### Input Topics
10 1. Camera intrinsics (`sensor_msgs/CameraInfo`)
11 1. Camera-LiDAR extrinsics (`tf`)
12 1. Object Detections results from a Vision Detector (`autoware_msgs/DetectedObjectArray`)
13 1. Object Detections results from a Range Detector (`autoware_msgs/DetectedObjectArray`)
14 
15 ### Output Topics
16 1. Fused Detected Objects (`autoware_msgs/DetectedObjectArray`) on the `/detection/fusion_tools/objects` topic.

18 ## Parameters
19 
20 Launch file available parameters:
21 
22 |Parameter| Type| Description|Default|
23 ----------|-----|--------|---|
24 |`detected_objects_range`|*String* |Name of the `DetectedObjectArray` topic to subscribe containing the detections on 3D space.|`/detection/lidar_objects`|
25 |`detected_objects_vision`|*String*|Name of the `DetectedObjectArray` topic to subscribe containing the detections on 2D space.|`/detection/vision_objects`|
26 |`camera_info_src`|*String*|Name of the CameraInfo topic that contains the intrinsic matrix for the Image.|`/camera_info`|
27 |`sync_topics`|*Bool*|Sync detection topics.|`false`|
28 |`min_car_dimensions`|*Array*|Sets the minimum dimensions for a car bounding box(width, height, depth) in meters.|`[2,2,4]`|
29 |`min_person_dimensions`|*Array*|Sets the minimum dimensions for a person bounding box (width, height, depth) in meters.|`[1,2,1]`|
30 |`min_truck_dimensions`|*Array*|Sets the minimum dimensions for a truck/bus bounding box (width, height, depth) in meters.|`[2,2,4.5]`|
31 |`overlap_threshold`|*float*|A number between 0.1 and 1.0 representing the area of overlap between the detections.|`0.5`|

33 ## Example of usage
34 
35 1. Launch a ground filter algorithm from the `Points Preprocessor` in the **Sensing** tab. (adjust the parameters to your vehicle setup).
36 1. Launch Calibration Publisher with the intrinsic and extrinsic calibration between the camera and the range sensor.
37 1. Launch a Vision Detector from the Computing tab (this should be publishing by default `/detectoion/vision_objects`).
38 1. Launch a Lidar Detector from the Computing tab (this should be publishing by default `/detectoion/lidar_objects`).
39 1. Launch this node.
40 1. Launch `rviz`, and add the topics shown above in the Output section.
41 
42 ## Notes
43 
44 * Detection on Image space should be performed on a **Rectified** Image, otherwise projection will be incorrect.
45 * The names of the classes are defined as in the COCO dataset.

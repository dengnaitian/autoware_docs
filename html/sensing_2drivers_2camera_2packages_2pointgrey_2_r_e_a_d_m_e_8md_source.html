1 # Autoware Point Grey Camera Drivers Package
2 
3 This package allows the capture of an image stream from Point Grey cameras.
4 It has been tested successfully with Grasshopper3 and LadyBug5 devices on both Ubuntu 14.04 and 16.04.
5 
6 ## Requirements
7 * FlyCapture SDK or Spinnaker SDK provided by Point Grey.
8 
9 ---
10 
11 ## Grasshopper3
12 
13 ### How to launch
14 * From a sourced terminal:\
15 `roslaunch autoware_pointgrey_drivers grasshopper3.launch`
16 * From Runtime manager:\
17 Sensing Tab -> Cameras -> PointGrey Grasshopper3
18 
19 ### Parameters available
20 
21 |Parameter| Type| Description|
22 ----------|-----|--------
23 |`fps`|*integer* |Defines the frames per second at which to attempt image stream acquisition.|
24 |`mode`|*integer*|Camera Mode - please check your camera for valid modes (0,1,2,...,31). |
25 |`format`|*string*|Pixel Format, which can be either `raw` or `rgb`. `raw` will publish the default bayer format according to your camera sensor. Both modes have 8 bits per pixel per channel.|
26 |`timeout`|*integer*|Timeout in miliseconds. Default 1000 ms.|
27 |`CalibrationFile`|*string*|Path to an Autoware-compatible calibration file to be published in the `camera_info` topic related to this camera.|

51 ## FLIR ADK
52 
53 Execute from `Autoware/ros` base path.
54 
55 1. `rosdep update`
56 1. `rosdep install -y --from-paths src --ignore-src --rosdistro $ROS_DISTRO`
57 1. `./catkin_make_release`
58 1. `./run`
59 1. Connect your camera
60 1. Confirm the camera has been detected using `v4l2-ctl --list-devices`
61 1. Select from *Sensing* tab / *Cameras* -> FLIR ADK
62 1. Press the `[config]` button and write the device name reported by `v4l2-ctl --list-devices`.
63 1. Click the *FLIR ADK* checkbox
64 1. Open Rviz and add image topic `/flir_adk/camera/image_raw`
65 
66 ### Parameters available
67 
68 |Parameter| Type| Description|
69 ----------|-----|--------
70 |`DEVICE`|*string* |Name of the system device in the form `/dev/videoX`. Obtain the correct name using `v4l2-ctl --list-devices`.|
71 |`FPS`|*integer*|Frame per second. Default 30. |
72 |`WIDTH`|*integer*|Image width of the stream (Default 640).|
73 |`HEIGHT`|*integer*|Image height of the camera (Default 512).|
74 |`NS`|*string*|Namespace to add as prefix. Default `flir_adk`.|
